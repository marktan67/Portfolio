I'm now a first-year BSIT student majoring in Cybersecurity at FEU Institute of Technology. Before college, I finished both Junior High and Senior High at Uno High School.

Outside of coursework, I split my attention between two very different worlds: quiet strategic thinking and high-pressure real-time response.
